What is Technical Community?

Technical Community is for a group of like minded people interested in a particular technology to come together, learn and discuss ideas.

What are the activities that can be done?

  • Quiz
  • Presentation
  • Hands on workshop
  • debate

How will it be useful?

First and foremost, opportunity is for every one to participate and involve. Second is mostly they have chosen their area of interest and so the passion will be more.
